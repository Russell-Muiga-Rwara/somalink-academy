About SomaLink

Bridging the Gap Between Education and Opportunity

SomaLink Futures is a student-focused platform helping young Africans discover career pathways, mentorship, scholarships and future-ready skills.

We believe every learner deserves access to guidance, opportunity and a clear pathway to professional success.

Our Mission

To empower African students with access to mentorship, scholarships, career guidance and learning resources.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital platform for career acceleration, mentorship and student development.

Our Story

Founded by students for students, SomaLink Futures was created to connect ambition with real opportunities.
