About SomaLink Futures

Who We Are

SomaLink Futures is a student-focused platform dedicated to helping young Africans discover career opportunities, mentorship programs, scholarships and future-ready skills.

We believe every student deserves access to quality guidance, opportunities, and a clear pathway to professional success.

Our Mission

To empower African students with access to mentorship, scholarships and career opportunities.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital platform for career acceleration and skills development.

Our Story

Founded by students for students, SomaLink Futures was created to bridge the gap between education and opportunity.
